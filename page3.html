Time Loop Theory


The time loop or temporal loop is a plot device in fiction whereby characters
re-experience a span of time which is repeated, sometimes more than once,
with some hope of breaking out of the cycle of repetition. In the loonaverse,
it is hinted right from the beginning of h1's creation that the moon is in cyclic, unstopping motion.
This members are forced to move through different births each time, to continue and re-continue the loop due to
h1's mishappenings. Later, during so what, we see hye 'burn the moon', or metaphorically, their very creation
to set the members free and break the running loop. One of the major consequences of this break causes
all members of the odd eye circle to lose their odd eyes.
